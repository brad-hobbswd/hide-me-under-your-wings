Dedication

This book is dedicated to every person who has carried strength for everyone else while quietly fighting hidden battles.

For every heart that smiled while hurting. For every soul that felt unseen. For every person who felt exhausted while continuing to give.

May these pages remind you that healing remains possible. May you discover refuge beneath the wings of Jesus. May peace replace fear. May strength rise again. May you always remember that you are seen, valued, loved, and never alone.
