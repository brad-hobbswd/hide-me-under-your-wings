Introduction

Life carries battles that many people never see. Some wounds happen openly. Others remain hidden beneath responsibilities, routines, ministry, expectations, and quiet smiles. Many people continue giving strength to everyone around them while secretly feeling empty themselves.

Ashley's journey represents countless hearts that have felt overwhelmed, exhausted, betrayed, misunderstood, and emotionally worn down while trying to remain strong. Through every storm she discovers a truth found throughout scripture. God provides shelter. God provides refuge. God provides a hiding place.

Psalm 91:4 declares: "He shall cover you with His feathers, and under His wings shall you trust."

These pages are an invitation into healing. An invitation toward peace. An invitation toward rest beneath the covering of Jesus. No matter how heavy life feels today, His wings remain wide enough to carry every burden.

Welcome to this journey.
