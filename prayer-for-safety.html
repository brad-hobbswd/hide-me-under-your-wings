Prayer For Safety

Lord Jesus,

Thank You for remaining my refuge and shelter through every battle and every storm. Thank You for covering me beneath Your wings and carrying me through moments when strength felt impossible.

Place Your hand upon my mind, heart, family, and every place my feet travel. Guard my thoughts. Guard my emotions. Guard my home. Guard every path before me.

Protect me from fear. Protect me from discouragement. Protect me from confusion. Protect me from words spoken against me.

Lead me beside still waters. Restore my soul. Give me strength for difficult days and peace during uncertain moments.

Psalm 91 declares You as my refuge and fortress. Today I stand upon that promise. I trust Your covering. I trust Your faithfulness. I trust Your love.

Thank You for never leaving me. Thank You for carrying me. Thank You for hiding me beneath Your wings.

In the name of Jesus, Amen.
