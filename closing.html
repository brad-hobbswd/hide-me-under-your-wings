Closing

Ashley's journey began in exhaustion, pain, and hidden battles. Fear surrounded her. Heartbreak surrounded her. Questions surrounded her. Yet every difficult season eventually led her beneath the wings of Jesus.

Every cave became preparation. Every storm became growth. Every tear carried purpose. Healing slowly unfolded in places nobody else could see.

"He shall cover you with His feathers, and under His wings shall you trust." Psalm 91:4

Life rarely changes overnight. Restoration often arrives gently. Strength often rises quietly. Jesus continues working beneath the surface even during seasons where progress feels invisible.

Your story is still being written. Your journey still carries purpose. Your hidden places still matter.

Green pastures remain ahead. Peace remains ahead. Healing remains ahead. Freedom remains ahead.

Continue trusting. Continue resting. Continue walking beneath His wings.

The same Jesus who carried Ashley remains faithful to carry you.
